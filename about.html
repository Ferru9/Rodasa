Rodasa Our Story

Who we are?

RODASA is a family business dedicated to satisfy industry necessities with high quality standards.

Want to hear some music while you read about our story?

We offer technical assistance for the installations of parts and supply of these. Selling and distribution of components, as well as services such as, training for the application and manipulation of the products we sell.

Focused on the commercialization of industrial spare parts with a fast, efficient and punctual service. Among the products we handle, we can mention: Bearings, Bearings, Belts, Retainers, Chains, pinions, couplings, lubricants and Industrial and Hydraulic Hoses.

Our Specialty

We have the capacity and knowledge to advise you in the industrial field, especially in the area of ​​bearings and power transmission elements.

History

Our company was born and created by Rene Ferrufino on El Salvador around the 1990s. The the company started growing until it affiliated with companies such as SKF, COVALCA, and EATON, The company continues to grow and have a great care for our costumers.
